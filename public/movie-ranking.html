Best Movies According to Karthik

My top three movies of all time

1. Memento

The movie has a simple concept, but the way it is portrayed is indeed a skill one should admire.

2. Interstellar

A true work of art having a base concept of science fiction as a tool used to tell about an emotional father-daughter story.

3. Shutter Island
